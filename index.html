Hello! We're Nonbinary Zone, an online community exclusively composed of more than a hundred non-binary, trans, and genderqueer people from around the world.


We aim to provide a safe and welcoming discussion and support space for non-binary and genderqueer people, and to advocate for ourselves and others by providing advice and feedback for anyone interested in improving inclusion of people who exist outside of the gender binary.


Our admins are Charlie Francis Cassidy, Damon Reece, and Maize Wallin.


Please email hello@nonbinary.zone for more information or an invitation link to our Discord community. You can also find us on Twitter.
