Discord Code of Conduct


Welcome to the Nonbinary Zone Discord community! It is a requirement of entry that you read everything here before moving onto other channels, so please take the time to do so.

Preamble

This Discord is strictly a space for individuals outside the gender binary - nonbinary, agender, genderqueer, and other genderweird identities are all welcome. We welcome individuals who are questioning their gender, but if you solely identify as a binary gender, we kindly request that you leave the server.

Community Expectations

The Nonbinary Zone Discord should be a safe place for everybody regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size/shape, class, race, age, or religion.

  1. As a member of this community, you agree that:
    • We are collectively and individually committed to safety and inclusivity.
    • We have zero tolerance for abuse, harassment, or discrimination.
    • We respect people's boundaries and identities, including their right to not reveal their gender identity outside of this Discord.
    • We refrain from using language that can be considered oppressive (systemically or otherwise), eg. sexist, racist, homophobic, non-binaryphobic, transphobic, ableist, classist, etc. - this includes (but is not limited to) various slurs.
    • We avoid using offensive topics as a form of humor.
  2. We actively work towards:
    • Being a safe community
    • Cultivating a network of support & encouragement for each other
    • Encouraging responsible and varied forms of expression
  3. We condemn:
    • Stalking, doxxing, or publishing private information
    • Threats of harm and harassment
    • Anything that compromises people's safety
    • Outing people without their permission (including referencing membership of this Discord in public)
These things are not okay.

If you say something that is found offensive, and you are called out on it, let's:
  • Listen without interruption.
  • Believe what the person is saying & do not attempt to disqualify what they have to say.
  • Ask for tips / help with avoiding making the offense in the future.
  • Apologize and ask forgiveness.
Failing to follow the community guidelines as described on this page carries consequences.

For minor infractions, you may be temporarily suspended from the Discord. Upon repeat offenses, or if the admins or community determine you are not acting in good faith, you may be removed or asked to leave permanently.
